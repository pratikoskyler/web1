Thoughts on Red Dead Redemption 2

I am EXTREMELY addicted to this game and I bought this game for $15 when it was on sale and let me tell you, it was one of the best decisions I have ever made in my life. The game got me hooked from the start and after 6 months of playing, I have finally beaten the story. That's how good it is. The story is absolutely top-tier to me, and I've spent over 500 hours (in total) playing the game. If you ever see it on sale, DON'T HESITATE TO PIRATE BUY IT.
